What are OERs?
OER stands for Open Educational Resources. They are freely accessible, openly licensed text, media, and other digital assets that are useful for teaching, learning, assessing, and research. The list we have available for access from this application ranges from GCF Learn Free to Khan Academy.
Why OERs?
OERs are free of charge to use and can significantly decrease student expenses on educational readings and content. Compared to textbooks, OERs help turn learning into an interactive and more interesting way for people to retain information. OERs can also circulate quickly, providing users with information they can access at any time.
Money Saved
Students do not have to pay to access OERs. A user needs a computer and internet connection to access these resources.
