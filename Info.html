• What are OERs?
    • OER stands for Open Educational Resources. They are freely accessible, openly licensed text, media, and other digital assets that are useful for teaching, learning, assessing, and research.
    • Some examples include University Literature, Digital Textbooks, Khan Academy,
  • Why OERs?
    • OERs are free of charge to use and can significantly decrease student expenses on educational readings and content.
    • OERs can circulate quickly.
  • Money Saved
    • Students do not have to pay for OERs.
